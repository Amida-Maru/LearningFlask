Introduction

Game Objectives

League of Legends is a MOBA (Multiplayer Online Battle Arena) strategy game. Two teams of 5 or 3 different heroes face each other, collecting gold and experience to use their advantage to win. The goal of the game is to destroy the most important point in the enemy base - the Nexus. This can be achieved by breaking through enemy fortifications or by forcing them to surrender.

League of Legends sets itself apart from the competition with its constant updates so that the game never loses its freshness. In addition, the game has over 140 heroes to choose from, and their pool is still expanding - whether by adding new ones or refreshing old ones. Each new player will find a dream hero for himself, with whom he most identifies his style of play.

Thanks to its attractiveness and mechanics, League of Legends has quickly become the most popular esports game in the world and brings together millions of players around the globe. Riot Games constantly supports professional games at every level - from the international LCS, LEC or LPL leagues, to more local ones, like the Polish Ultraliga. The studio also collaborates with the most popular Streamer and Youtubers from around the globe, granting them additional perks and skins to give away, and even closer to the headquarters, offering testing of new and experimental content long before the premiere for official servers.
