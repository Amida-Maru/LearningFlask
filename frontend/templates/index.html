ESPORTS IN LEAGUE OF LEGENDS

The goal of this website is to present fundamental information about League of Legends and its esports ecosystem in an accessible and straightforward way.

Therefore, even if you have never played the game and have not participated in any esports events yet, do not worry as the "ABOUT GAME" and "ABOUT ESPORTS" sections have you covered! Did you know that the content of this website has been designed with complete beginners in mind? You can expect only easy to follow articles supplemented by beginner friendly videos and references to helpful 3rd party websites under the "USEFUL WEBSITES" section. In other words, everything to get you started exploring and enjoying the incredible world of esports in League of Legends in no time!
