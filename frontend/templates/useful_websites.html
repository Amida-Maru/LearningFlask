Introduction

Esports in League of Legends

Lolpross.gg is a 3rd party website that unlike lolfandom.com and lolsesport.com focuses on the esports players, their social media, and their personal statistics, rather than on the more general aspects of esports related to League of Legends. Because of these unique features, the website has grown in popularity within the community in the recent years, filling the major functionality gap and providing a more comprehensive esports experience for the community members.

Introduction

Website Lol.fandom.com

Lol.fandom.com is a 3rd party website that is part of fandom.com, which was established back in 2004 by Jimmy Wales and Angela Beesly Starling. It uses the core technologies developed for Wikipedia that were modified and further enhanced to suit the needs of gamers, film enthusiasts and IT-oriented users. Similar to Wikipedia, the content presented on the platform is created by various contributors, therefore not all the published information can be considered as a reliable source. For the esports in League of Legends purposeslol.fandom.com is an excellent source of information when it comes to esports event schedules, tournaments history, players, esports organisation, and more...

Introduction

Website Lolesports.com

Lolesports.com is the official esports website created by the developers of League of Legends, Riot Games. It contains information about esports news pertaining to League of Legends with an emphasis on the current and upcoming tournaments, as well as the results of the recent matches. In essence, lolesport.com provides a similar set of functionalities to lol.fandom.com where users can navigate through different esports sections however, with more limited content available.
